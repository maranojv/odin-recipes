Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Rolled Oats
  • 1 Cup Almond Milk
  • 1/2 Cup Greek Yogurt
  • 1/4 Cup Peanut Butter

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients and stir until combined
  2. Chill in refrigerator for at least 6 hours ( or preferably overnight)
  3. Enjoy with your favorite topping
